Pretec Commerce Platform
Full Platform Specification
The definitive specification for the Pretec B2B e-commerce platform — covering all integrations, services, functional capabilities, authentication, configuration, and cross-cutting concerns.
Audience: Pretec and Geta Prepared by: Geta Date: June 2026 Status: Complete — open items tracked in §9

The Pretec webshop is a B2B e-commerce platform built on the Mosaik storefront (Next.js / React), powered by RamBase ERP as the source of truth, with Struct for product enrichment, Sanity for editorial content, and a Geta-built Pretec Service API for live price, cart, quote, and order operations. Harmony synchronises data between systems on a 10-minute schedule.

This document consolidates every agreed decision across all specification areas. Decisions are confirmed; genuinely open items requiring Pretec input are highlighted in yellow throughout and collected in the open items summary.

Section 1

I-1
Product & Category Sync — RamBase → Struct via Harmony
Products and categories originate in RamBase and are synced into Struct for enrichment.
RamBase→ Harmony→ Struct — scheduled delta, every 10 min
Key decisions
PropertyValue
Sync cadenceEvery 10 minutes, delta (changed records only)
DirectionOne-way: RamBase → Struct. No write-back.
System of recordRamBase owns: SKU, status, UoM, category. Struct owns: display name, descriptions, images, attributes, SEO.
Product display nameStruct display name overrides RamBase ERP name on the storefront.
Category sourceRamBase (authoritative). Up to 4 levels: Segment → K1 → K2 → K3. Enriched in Struct.
DeactivationRamBase status change synced to Struct; downstream catalog sync (I-2) removes from listings. URL remains accessible with "Ikke lenger tilgjengelig".
Enrichment preservationHarmony preserves Struct-owned fields (descriptions, images, display name) during RamBase updates.
Initial loadFull load of all active products and categories at go-live; delta thereafter.

Enriched, publish-flagged products and categories are pushed from Struct to the Storefront Catalog.
Struct→ Harmony Catalog Sync→ Storefront Catalog — every 10 min
Key decisions
PropertyValue
Publish triggerExplicit "Published for web" flag in Struct — Pretec marks each product ready. Active in RamBase AND published in Struct = visible on storefront.
Sync cadenceEvery 10 minutes, delta
Deactivated productRemoved from listings and search. URL stays accessible showing "Ikke lenger tilgjengelig" — no 404, no redirect.
